# Case Study (approved): Marketplace — Seller Onboarding Trust

A top-20 US marketplace added Predictive DocV as a risk-conditioned step-up in seller onboarding, alongside already-deployed Sigma Identity Fraud.

## Approved results
- Fraudulent seller accounts down 71% quarter over quarter.
- Seller signup completion recovered to within 2% of pre-step-up baseline because only ~14% of signups (the risky cohort) saw the document flow.
- Median added latency for stepped-up users: under 1 second for document decisioning.

## Usage policy
- Approved for marketplace and platform prospects; strongest for expansion deals where a Sigma model is already deployed.
- Persona fit: Trust & Safety leadership (fraud-ring framing), Engineering (latency + integration framing).
