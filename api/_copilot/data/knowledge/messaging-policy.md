# Messaging & Claims Policy (internal)

## Hard rules
- Quantified claims (percentages, dollar figures, latency numbers) may only be used if they appear verbatim in an approved product page or case study in this knowledge base.
- Case-study results must be framed as one customer's outcome, never as a guarantee or average.
- Competitive comparisons must not name competitors in customer-facing text; position on capabilities only.
- Pricing, discounts, and contractual terms are never generated by the copilot — escalate to the deal desk.

## Tone
- Lead with the customer's stated problem, not the product name.
- One clear call to action per message; suggested working-session length is 30 minutes.
