# Device Risk (Device & Behavior Intelligence)

Device, network, behavioral, velocity, and IP signals for detecting account takeover,
credential stuffing, bot attacks, and login risk — at onboarding and at every high-risk
moment across the account lifecycle.

## When to lead with it
- Account-takeover or credential-stuffing spikes at login.
- Manual review queues overloaded by flagged sessions.
- "Build vs buy" conversations about in-house device fingerprinting: position the cross-
  customer signal network no single company can replicate internally.

## Approved claims
- Detects risky devices and automation patterns before credentials are even submitted.
- Signals feed the same RiskOS decisioning as identity and document checks, so login risk,
  onboarding risk, and compliance run through one workflow.

## Proof points (approved)
- Crypto-exchange case study: a top-10 US exchange cut account-takeover losses 64% in one
  quarter while reducing manual login reviews by half, by step-upping only sessions with
  risky device signals.

## Usage policy
- Strongest fit: crypto, fintech, marketplaces, and any login-heavy consumer platform.
- Persona fit: CISO (attack framing), Risk Operations (review-queue framing), CFO (loss
  and headcount framing).
