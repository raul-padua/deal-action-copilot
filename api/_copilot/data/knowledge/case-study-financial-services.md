# Case Study (approved): Regional Bank — Digital Account Opening

A US regional bank launching digital account opening deployed Sigma Synthetic Fraud and Socure Verify inside a RiskOS onboarding workflow.

## Approved results
- Manual review reduced from 17% to 6% of applications.
- Auto-approval rate up 9 points with no measured increase in fraud losses.
- Application abandonment down 12% after step-up was limited to high-risk cohorts only.

## Usage policy
- Approved for financial-services prospects in evaluation stages and later.
- Do not quote results as guarantees; frame as "results one customer achieved."
- Persona fit: Head/VP of Fraud (loss + review volume framing), VP Digital Banking (conversion + abandonment framing).
