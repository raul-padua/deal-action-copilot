# Workforce Verification & Continuous Screening

Extends identity assurance beyond onboarding: verify employees, contractors, and gig
workers at hire, then re-verify at meaningful moments (shift start, device change,
payout changes) to catch account sharing, account selling, and post-hire identity drift.

## When to lead with it
- Gig platforms detecting account sharing: verified workers handing accounts to
  unverified people after onboarding.
- Compliance regimes requiring ongoing (not point-in-time) workforce assurance.
- Existing Socure Verify customers: continuous screening is a natural expansion because
  the identity baseline from onboarding is already established.

## Approved claims
- Re-verification reuses the onboarding identity record, so returning workers see a
  low-friction selfie check rather than a full re-enrollment.
- The same trust problem covered at customer onboarding applies to the workforce; one
  platform covers both.

## Proof points (approved)
- Delivery-platform case study: periodic selfie re-verification on high-risk accounts cut
  detected account-sharing incidents by 78% within two months, with a re-check that takes
  workers under 10 seconds.

## Usage policy
- Persona fit: Operations (fraud and quality framing), Compliance (regulatory framing).
- For early-stage conversations without a budget owner, recommend a problem-sizing
  workshop before any commercial proposal.
